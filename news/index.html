MINDAI project funded by the Spanish Ministry of Science

Our project MINDAI has been awarded funding under the Proyectos de Generación de Conocimiento 2025 call (AEI). Over the next four years, we will develop AI tools for multimodal single-cell data integration and build a comprehensive atlas of the mouse cortex.

July 2026

Lab at FENS 2026: DECAG and NeuroPSM

Our lab is at FENS 2026 this week! Caterina Fuses presents DECAG, a deep generative model for inferring somatic CAG-repeat expansion from single-cell RNA-seq in Huntington’s disease, and Berta Ros & David Bonet present NeuroPSM, a transformer-based model for predicting neural responses to optogenetic photostimulation. Proud of both teams!
